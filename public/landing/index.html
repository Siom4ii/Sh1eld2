About the Project

SHIELD Program: Index System

SHIELD will be a web-based information system capable of supporting Katuparan Center which will act as the center for development for the former rebels. It will serve as a site for system administration supervising the former rebels along with their socio-economic challenges in the communities where these former rebels are reintegrating back into society and are accessing the livelihood support through the different government agencies.

This system will allow for different government agencies to identify and resolve issues and problems in a particular conflict affected area. GeoTagging will be a feature of the proposed system in the challenge of adding geographical identification data to the conflict-affected areas that the former rebels lived in. The geographical identification data will consist of coordinates by calling the latitude and longitude. Descriptive analytics will also be a part of the proposed system to visualize the reintegration progress for the geographic areas as provided by the multiple government agencies provided to the former rebels by data analysis.
